  • Salwaat Takhseem is a mobile application designed to simplify Salwaat distribution process carried out during month of Shehre Rajabul Asab. The app helps admins, sub-admins, and users manage and track Salawaat distribution across different mohallahs.
  • Admins can manage all mohallahs and users, sub-admins can manage members within their assigned mohallah, and users can easily book, view schedules, and stay updated — all from a single unified platform
